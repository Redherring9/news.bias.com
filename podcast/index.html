What Will Trigger the Next World War? With Peter Apps

Intelligence Squared GB

Defence commentator Peter Apps maps the converging flashpoints that could trigger the next world war—from Ukraine and Taiwan to cyber warfare and space militarization. Drawing on reporting from Washington, Moscow, Beijing and conflict zones, Apps argues that the foundations of a new kind of global conflict are already being laid. This is a sobering, evidence-based exploration of the geopolitical fault lines that matter most right now.

How we pick

Each morning our pipeline scans recent episodes from 22 curated shows spanning technology, science, history, culture, politics, and long-form journalism. An AI model scores each episode for intellectual depth, timeliness, and broad appeal — with a deliberate bias toward episodes that engage with something happening right now.

Unlike the news feed (which favours calm and constructive stories), the podcast pick is chosen to be compelling and current — strong opinions, lively debate, and challenging ideas are all welcome here, provided the content is substantive and evidence-based.

We avoid repeating the same show within a two-week window so you get genuine variety. Episodes must have been published in the last seven days, and run between 10 and 180 minutes.
